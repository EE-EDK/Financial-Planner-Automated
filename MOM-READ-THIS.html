üí° Your Budget Has 3 Parts

1. Monthly Expenses (Columns C-N in Excel)

Things you spend money on every month:

  • Auto, Car Insurance, Utilities
  • Medical, Clothes, Vacation
  • And all your other expenses

2. Savings Goals (Columns O-S in Excel)

Money you're setting aside for specific things:

  • Mom's money, Interest
  • Mower saving, Furniture Saving
  • Extra TSP

3. Account Tracking (Columns T-V in Excel)

Keep track of your money:

  • Savings - How much in savings
  • Deposits - Money added
  • Balance - Current balance
